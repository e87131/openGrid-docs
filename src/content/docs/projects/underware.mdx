## About

Underware is a cable management system by Hands on Katie. You can watch the [original introduction video by Hands on Katie]https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0TT96b98YZY) and the [2.0 update video by Hands on Katie](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W33lTKwrfCI) to get a general understanding on what Underware looks like and whether it's the right fit for your setup.

Documentation for Underware is available on Hands on Katie's website:
* [Underware](https://handsonkatie.com/underware/)
* [Underware 2.0 Update](https://handsonkatie.com/underware-2-0-the-made-to-measure-collection/)

## Resources

You can use the collections on printables or the generators on makerworld to get the parts you need without designing them yourself.
* [openGrid Underware on printables](https://www.printables.com/model/1214427-underware-for-opengrid-manage-your-cables-lite-ly)
* [openGrid Underware on makerworld](https://makerworld.com/en/models/1175037-underware-for-opengrid)
* [openGrid Underware generator on makerworld](https://makerworld.com/en/models/1329404-underware-for-opengrid-customizer-beta)

If you want to use existing models, and just need openGrid compatible mounts, check out the page on [Modeling resources](https://www.opengrid.world/guides/modeling/).
